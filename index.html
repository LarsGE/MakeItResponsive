ABOUT US

We are a Inova, a newly formed company who focuses on frugal innovation. Our goal is to help other innovative companies to do more with less. Through our innovation camps we can share and spread our expierences in order to reach our goal.

INNOVATING TODAY

Shaping tomorrow.

Inova hosts innovation camps all around the world. We have gathered thousands of innovative ideas ranging from cars made of bamboo that uses fireworks as fuel, to a lawn mower that can trim bushes and small trees. Some of our ideas get made into life like our 20 dollars graphics cards that are made out of plastic and teeth picks.
