ABOUT US

We are a Inova, a newly formed company who focuses on frugal innovation. Our goal is to help other innovative companies to do more with less. Through our innovation camps we can share and spread our expierences in order to reach our goal.

INNOVATION CAMPS

Inova hosts innovation camps all around the world. We have gathered thousands of innovative ideas ranging from cars made of bamboo that uses fireworks as fuel, to a lawn mower that can trim bushes and small trees. Some of our ideas get made into life like our 20 dollars graphics cards that are made out of plastic and toothpicks. These graphics cards use dirty water as the electrical conductor and as a cooling system.

In our innovation camps you will get insight in how our amazing ideas came to life. You will also learn about the process and how to work in groups. In our innovation camps there will also be arranged a competition, where groups of up to 6-12 people will come up with a innovative idea to the problem. The problem will be relevant to the participants work field. In the end there will be a winner, who will receive various rewards.
